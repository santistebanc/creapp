A Simple Multiple-Choice Quiz Game
TriviaApp is a simple quiz game about various categories of topics. Geography, History, Science, Language, Medicine/Biology, Culture, Movies, Technology, Sports, and General Knowledge. It presents the player a short question followed by 4 different choices of which one is correct and the player has to select it to pass to the next question. It is fun, entertaining and at the same educative! Try it here or download it free to your smartphone here.
  • Multiple choice
  • Various categories
  • Anonymous scoring
  • Questions browsing and search
  • Regular updates
  • Completely Free!
Requirements:
Compatible with iPhone and iPod touch. Requires iPhone OS 2.2 or later. WiFi, Edge, or 3G network connection sometimes required.
Requirements:
Requires Android 2.3 and higher. WiFi, Edge, or 3G network connection sometimes required.

About

TriviaApp is free, available for all major mobile platforms: iOS, Android, Blackberry, WindowsPhone, Symbian, webOS, and for any Web Browser on a computer. To download this app for mobile platforms visit here. Or visit the on-web app here.

This app was created for the 'Ingenieria de Software' class final project. This app will never use, record or ask for any of your personal information. Only your scores may be saved anonymously to our database.

Stay put for new updates and new questions being uploaded.
